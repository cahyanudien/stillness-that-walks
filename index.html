Cahyanudien spends his nights weaving logic in the shadows — building for thousands while remaining unseen. This book is what surfaces when the code falls silent.

github/cahyanudien

Silence That Walks is a collection of poems born from a quiet space — about a journey of the soul that is never truly finished. Written in three parts: Root, Growing, and Sky, this book invites readers to reflect on loss, self-discovery, and the courage to keep walking even without a certain direction. It is not meant to be read in haste, but to be experienced in silence — where every line may feel as if it belongs to you.
